# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: July 2026_

## Overview

FileKit is committed to protecting your privacy.

FileKit performs file management and analytics operations locally on your computer. Your files remain under your control at all times.

## Information Collection

FileKit does **not** collect, store, or transmit personal information.

The application does not require user accounts or authentication.

## File Access

FileKit only accesses files and folders that you explicitly select.

The application never scans, modifies, or deletes files outside the locations chosen by the user.

## Network Communication

FileKit does not transmit your files or personal information to external servers.

Network access, if used (for example, checking for software updates or downloading releases), is limited to official FileKit resources.

## Analytics

FileKit does not collect usage analytics, telemetry, or tracking data.

## Cookies

FileKit does not use cookies.

## Third-Party Components

FileKit is built using open-source technologies, including Electron.js, React, Java, and other community-maintained libraries.

These components may have their own licenses and privacy practices.

## Data Security

All file operations are performed locally on your device.

Users are responsible for maintaining backups of important data before performing file management operations.

## Changes to this Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as FileKit evolves.

Material changes will be reflected in future releases and repository updates.

## Contact

For privacy-related questions, please open an issue on the official GitHub repository.
